Change Donation settings in CotEditor on Mac

Use Donation settings in CotEditor to donate to the CotEditor project or change the settings related to continuous support.

To change these settings in the CotEditor app on your Mac, choose CotEditor > Settings, then click Donation.

OptionDescription
Continuous support A continuous donation to the CotEditor project. The subsription will automatically be renewed yearly. To stop the subscription, go to the App Store from the “Manage subscriptions” link and cancel the subscription there.
Badge type Select the badge to display on the status bar during continuous support.
One-time donation A one-time donation to the CotEditor project. You can send it multiple times but there is no particular reward like the coffee badge by the continuous support.
  • The donation uses the in-app purchase on the App Store provided by Apple. This feature is available only in CotEditor downloaded from the App Store.
  • The donation buttons are available only when an internet connection exists.
  • The donations made here will be made to the individual developer.
